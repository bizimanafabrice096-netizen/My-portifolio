About

I am passionate about IoT solutions, connecting devices to improve everyday life. Rooted in Muhura and proud to represent Kigali, I focus on building smart systems that merge technology with community needs. My vision is to empower homes, farms, and cities with connected equipment that improves efficiency, safety, and sustainability.

The IoT Equipment initiative is not just about devices — it’s about creating meaningful experiences where technology serves people. From smart sensors to intelligent networks, every project reflects innovation and cultural pride.

Smart Home Sensors

IoT devices for monitoring temperature, humidity, and security. These sensors integrate with mobile apps to provide real-time alerts, ensuring comfort and safety for families.

Connected Agriculture

IoT irrigation systems that track soil moisture and weather conditions. Farmers can optimize water usage, reduce costs, and increase crop yields while protecting the environment.

Smart Health Monitoring

Wearable IoT devices that monitor vital signs such as heart rate and oxygen levels. Designed to support preventive healthcare and connect patients with doctors remotely.

Smart City Lighting

Intelligent streetlights that adjust brightness based on traffic and pedestrian activity. This reduces energy consumption and enhances urban safety.

Industrial IoT Systems

Connected equipment for factories that track machine performance, predict maintenance needs, and improve productivity through data-driven insights.
